  • Limit Fonts

    Use different fonts in your design, but avoid overusing them. Stick to three or fewer fonts, ensuring they work well together.Thank you.

  • Adjust Text Size and Height

    Overlay text on images creatively. Change line height and spacing to create a “box effect.” Achieve pleasing placement and symmetry for visual impact.

  • Contrasting Fonts

    Pair bold fonts with cursive or romantic ones to convey mood and draw attention1.Avoid cramming too many words into one line; aim for 50 to 60 characters per line.

    Overloading your design with too much text can turn off your audience, Combining too many fonts can make your design look disorganized and unprofessional1.
